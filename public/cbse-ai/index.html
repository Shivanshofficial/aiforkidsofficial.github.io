Artificial Intelligence for CBSE Students

add_link


CBSE started introducing artificial intelligence from class 6 onwards as a 6th subject for all the Cbse Schools in 2019 and the partnership with Intel for the Different resources and training programs for teachers.

This initiative for introducing AI in CBSE schools is creating a great opportunity for students to interact with the technologies.
